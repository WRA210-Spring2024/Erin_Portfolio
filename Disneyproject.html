Dinsey Cruise Line Campaign

A mock-up campaign to increase audience engagement was tasked within my class, Content Strategy. As a group project, we chose to create a contest for potential customers to win a special vacation with Disney Cruise Line. My individual tasks included establishing the style guide, tone/language, campaign timeline, and reflection.

Style Guide

We started out developing a style guide with components such as color schemes, voice, and font-faces matching the Disney name to implement in our campaign.

Tone and Language

After introducing the purpose of the campaign and launch strategies, my role of establishing and implementing the tone and language for campaign posts followed. I noted on our slide deck to put emphasis on utuilzing exciting, upbeat wording to capture the magical voice the Disney brand characterizes.

Campaign Process Timeline

Another key repsonsibility I took initiative on was creating a campaign timeline to stay on track and see, in writing what needed to be fulfilled. Components such as announcements, postings, and post-campaign evaluation are listed as tasks in a given week period.
